BIOGRAPHY

Crystal Marshall was born in St. Andrew, Jamaica in 1982. The Jamaican born artist attended Maryland Institute College of Art for her Post-Baccalaureate Certificate and the University of Michigan for his MFA. She has been in a number of exhibitions including the 2013 Forward Arts Foundation Artist of the year Retrospective in Atlanta, GA, 2012 “Outward Reach: 9 Jamaican Photography and New Media Artists" at the Art Museum of the Americas in Washington, DC, 2011 “African Continuum” at the United Nations Gallery in New York, 2011 Mover and Shaker show at Museum of Contemporary Art Georgia and the 2010 and 2014 Jamaica Biannual. “Outstanding Student Achievement in Contemporary Sculpture Award”. Crystal Marshall is currently a professor at Morehouse College.
